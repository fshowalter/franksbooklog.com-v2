Behold the Void

by
Philip Fracassi
2017 | Collection
on Sat, May 7, 2022

An accomplished debut collection from Fracassi. “Altar” shines with “Mandala” a strong second. Not a below average entry in the bunch. And I loved the author’s notes section, where Fracassi grants insight into each story’s origin (and confirms my thoughts on the ending of “Mandala”).
